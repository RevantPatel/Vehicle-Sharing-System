How It Works

Learn how to rent a car or list your vehicle on VSS

Renting a Car on VSS

1

Search for Available Cars

Browse our extensive selection of cars based on your location, dates, and preferences. Filter by car type, price range, and features to find the perfect match for your needs.

Pro Tip

Book in advance for better availability and rates, especially during peak seasons and holidays.

2

Book Your Car

Once you've found the right car, select your pickup and drop-off dates and times. Review the car details, pricing, and rental terms before confirming your booking.

Pro Tip

Add insurance coverage for peace of mind during your rental period.

3

Verify Your Identity

For security purposes, we require all renters to verify their identity. This includes providing your driving license details and a selfie with your license for verification.

Pro Tip

Complete your profile and verification before booking to speed up the process.

4

Pick Up the Car

Meet the car owner at the agreed pickup location. Together, you'll inspect the car and document its condition before you drive away.

Pro Tip

Take photos of the car before driving away to document its condition.

5

Enjoy Your Trip

Drive and enjoy your rental car. Our 24/7 customer support is available if you need any assistance during your rental period.

Pro Tip

Treat the car as if it were your own to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

6

Return the Car

Return the car to the agreed location at the scheduled time. The owner will inspect the car, and you'll complete the return process together.

Pro Tip

Refill the fuel to the same level as when you received the car to avoid additional charges.

7

Leave a Review

Share your experience by rating the car and the owner. Your feedback helps our community and improves the service for everyone.

Pro Tip

Honest, detailed reviews help other renters make informed decisions.

Listing Your Car on VSS

1

Create Your Listing

Sign up and create a detailed listing for your car. Include information about the make, model, year, features, and high-quality photos to attract potential renters.

Pro Tip

Clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles help your listing stand out.

2

Set Your Availability & Pricing

Define when your car is available for rent and set your daily rate. You can also offer discounts for weekly or monthly rentals to attract longer bookings.

Pro Tip

Research similar cars in your area to set competitive pricing.

3

Verify Your Car & Documents

Upload your car registration, insurance documents, and other required information for verification. This helps build trust with potential renters.

Pro Tip

Ensure your insurance policy covers peer-to-peer car sharing.

4

Receive Booking Requests

Once your listing is live, you'll start receiving booking requests from potential renters. Review their profiles and rental history before accepting.

Pro Tip

Respond to requests promptly to increase your booking rate.

5

Hand Over Your Car

Meet the renter at the agreed location, verify their identity, and inspect the car together. Document the car's condition and fuel level before handing over the keys.

Pro Tip

Create a checklist for the handover process to ensure nothing is missed.

6

Get Your Car Back

Meet the renter at the end of the rental period to inspect the car and complete the return process. Check for any damages or issues that need to be addressed.

Pro Tip

Compare the car's condition with the pre-rental photos to identify any changes.

7

Get Paid

Receive your payment automatically within 24-48 hours after the rental ends. The rental amount (minus our service fee) will be transferred directly to your bank account.

Pro Tip

Keep track of your earnings and set aside funds for car maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my car protected during rentals?

Yes, all cars listed on VSS are covered by our comprehensive insurance policy during rental periods. This is in addition to your existing insurance and covers damages, accidents, and theft.

What happens if the car breaks down during a rental?

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance for all rentals. If a breakdown occurs, the renter should contact our support team immediately, and we'll arrange for assistance. Mechanical issues that are not the renter's fault are covered by the car owner.

Can I cancel my booking/listing?

Yes, both renters and car owners can cancel bookings. However, cancellation policies apply, and fees may be charged depending on how close to the rental start date the cancellation occurs. Refer to our cancellation policy for detailed information.

How is the security deposit handled?

A security deposit is held at the start of each rental and is fully refundable if the car is returned in the same condition. The deposit amount varies based on the car's value and is clearly displayed before booking.

What if the renter returns the car late?

Late returns are subject to additional hourly or daily charges. Renters should notify the owner and VSS support as soon as possible if they anticipate returning the car late.
