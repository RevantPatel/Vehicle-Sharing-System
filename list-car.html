Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn by listing my car?

Your earnings depend on your car's make, model, condition, and location. On average, car owners on VSS earn between ₹15,000 to ₹30,000 per month. Luxury cars can earn even more.

Is my car protected during rentals?

Yes, all cars listed on VSS are covered by our comprehensive insurance policy during rental periods. This is in addition to your existing insurance and covers damages, accidents, and theft.

Who can rent my car?

Only verified users with valid driving licenses can rent your car. We conduct thorough background checks on all renters. You also have the option to approve or decline rental requests.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed automatically after each rental period. The rental amount (minus our service fee) is transferred directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours after the rental ends.

What if my car gets damaged?

If your car is damaged during a rental, our insurance will cover the repairs. You'll need to report the damage within 24 hours of the rental ending, and we'll guide you through the claims process.
