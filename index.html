About VSS

VSS is a peer-to-peer car rental marketplace that connects car owners with people who need a vehicle. Our mission is to make car rental more accessible, affordable, and sustainable.

Founded in 2023, we've helped thousands of car owners earn extra income from their vehicles while providing renters with a wide variety of affordable options.
